Making oreo milkshake at home is easy you just need few ingredients,blend them and then enjoy your delicious oreo milkshake

𝙄𝙉𝙂𝙍𝙀𝘿𝙄𝙀𝙉𝙏𝙎


  • Oreo biscuits

  • 1 Cup vanilla ice cream

  • 1 Table spoon sugar

  • 1 Cup milk

  • 1 Table spoon chocolate sauce(OPTIONAL)


𝗠𝗘𝗧𝗛𝗢𝗗

  1. Roughly break the oreo biscuits and put them in a bowl

  2. Now,pour milk and chocolate syrup to a blender.Also add oreo cookies and ice cream to the blender.blend well all the ingredients until combined completely

  3. Crush and crumble the oreo cookies that you have put in a bowl

  4. Pour the prepared milkshake into a glass.Finally,top the milkshake with the crumbled oreo cookies or put an oreo biscuit on the top of the milkshake And

  5. ENJOY
